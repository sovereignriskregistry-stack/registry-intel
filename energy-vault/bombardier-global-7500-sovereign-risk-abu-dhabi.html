  • Avionics Interdependency: The 7500 utilizes advanced fly-by-wire architecture. In the event of localized sovereign "GPS spoofing" or electronic warfare (EW) interference common in 2026 maritime/regional tensions, the aircraft’s Enhanced Vision System (EVS) faces significant integrity risks.
  • Operational Duty Cycles: Frequent short-cycle operations in high-ambient-temperature environments accelerate TBO (Time Between Overhaul) degradation for the Passport 20 engine compressors.
