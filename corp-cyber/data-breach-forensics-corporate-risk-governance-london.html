III. Technical Forensic Mandates: Beyond EDR/XDR

Standard Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) is insufficient for the 2026 threat vector. We observe an increase in "Living-off-the-Land" (LotL) attacks where adversaries utilize legitimate administrative tools (PowerShell, WMI, Azure AD Connect) to bypass legacy security stack detection. Corporate risk governance must now enforce:

  1. Immutable Log Integrity: Real-time replication of forensic logs to write-once-read-many (WORM) storage.
  2. Memory-Forensic Readiness: Implementing volatile memory capture protocols to identify fileless malware that escapes traditional disk-based forensic scrutiny.
  3. Supply Chain Verification: Mandatory forensic auditing of third-party SaaS APIs, which now represent 64% of documented lateral movement vectors in London-based financial services.
