Operators must account for "Nuclear Damage Liability" (NDL) insurance premiums, which are currently trending at 350-500 basis points above standard critical infrastructure coverage.
  • Cyber-Kinetic Insurance Premiums: Due to the sensitivity of SMR control systems (ICS/SCADA), underwriters are demanding higher premiums for "Air-Gap Integrity" audits. High-CPC insurance products focusing on business interruption due to cyber-interference are currently seeing record demand, with premiums projected to fluctuate based on the specific technological vendor’s cybersecurity pedigree.

This is a deliberate "de-risking" investment designed to lower long-term liability premiums.
  • Contingent Business Interruption (CBI): Assets are susceptible to regional flux. Calculating the Cost of Capital requires a sensitivity analysis that factors in a 20% buffer for supply chain disruptions, specifically regarding rare-earth components and specialized high-assay low-enriched uranium (HALEU) fuel cycles.
