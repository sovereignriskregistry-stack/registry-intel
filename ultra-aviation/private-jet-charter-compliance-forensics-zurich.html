Regulatory Liability

Liability in private jet chartering is rarely straightforward. Many clients operate under the misconception that the broker assumes the operator's liability. In the eyes of the law, the "flight department" or the individual entity signing the charter agreement holds ultimate vicarious liability for regulatory non-compliance. Our forensic audit process examines:

  • AOC Verification: Confirming the Air Operator Certificate (AOC) status to prevent illegal "grey market" charters.
  • Maintenance Tracing: Verifying that the aircraft’s airworthiness directive (AD) compliance is logged in real-time, not retroactively.
  • Crew Licensing: Ensuring that pilot fatigue management protocols—mandated by EASA—are rigorously followed.
