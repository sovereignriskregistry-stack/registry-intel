Strategic Consultation Checklist

Before entering the ELC market, BioTech operations teams must perform rigorous due diligence. Use the following checklist to ensure compliance and mission success:

  • Infrastructure Verification: Does the charter operator possess active temperature-controlled handling units (Envirotainers) compatible with the specific aircraft tail number?
  • Regulatory Synchronization: Have you secured the necessary Swissmedic and international import/export permits specific to the 2026 Genetic Modification (GM) handling protocols?
  • The "Shadow Charter" Contingency: For mission-critical CRISPR samples, do you have a secondary "dead-head" flight on standby? Empty legs are subject to cancellation if the primary owner changes their schedule; always mandate a primary-backup protocol.
  • Chain of Custody (CoC): Are there provisions for a specialized bio-courier to accompany the payload? Relying on pilots for cold-chain monitoring is a high-risk operational oversight.
  • Tarmac Protocols: Ensure the Fixed-Base Operator (FBO) at both origin and destination provides climate-controlled, secure tarmac transfer services, bypassing general cargo warehouses.
