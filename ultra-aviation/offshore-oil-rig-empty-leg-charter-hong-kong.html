  • Liability and Insurance Implications: Unlike standard commercial travel, offshore rig charters operate in high-risk zones. Standard aviation hull and liability insurance often exclude specific maritime transit corridors. Executives must negotiate "Broad Form" coverage that addresses multi-modal transport (air-to-sea transfer) in the South China Sea.

Compliance with the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) Annex 14 standards is non-negotiable for 2026 operations. Failure to verify the Air Operator Certificate (AOC) regarding specific maritime endorsements could expose the firm to catastrophic legal liability.
