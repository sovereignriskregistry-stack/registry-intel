Regulatory Liability Framework

Liability in Riyadh is governed by the Saudi Ministry of Energy and the Saudi Ports Authority (MAWANI) for offshore assets. Key considerations include:

  • Employee Injury Claims: Navigating GOSI (General Organization for Social Insurance) versus private international claims under the Saudi Labor Law.
