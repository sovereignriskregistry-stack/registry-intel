Expert Analysis

Regulatory Liability

Under the jurisdiction of maritime law and localized state mandates, corporate entities face a "strict liability" framework. Forensic analysis is the primary defense against piercing the corporate veil. By deploying specialized forensic teams, stakeholders can establish a 'defensible record' of compliance. This protects directors and investors from individual liability in the event of an OPA-90 (Oil Pollution Act) trigger or a Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) audit failure.

Cost Structures

Forensic compliance programs are best viewed as capital preservation instruments. Costs generally break down into three tiers:

  • Continuous Monitoring (OpEx): Deployment of remote sensor telemetry and routine digital forensic health checks.

  • Litigation Support: Expert witness testimony and evidentiary preservation, which are billed at premium consultancy rates due to the specialized nature of maritime-energy law in New York courts.
